Frequently Asked Questions

Audio does not play when I tap the button.
Make sure your device is not on silent/mute. On the very first launch, the current month's audio downloads in the background — please allow a moment and try again.

The app shows a white screen on launch.
This is normal on the first launch while the app prepares audio content. Subsequent launches are much faster.

How much storage does the app use?
Audio files are delivered as on-demand resources and are downloaded in the background over time. Only the current month's audio (~130 MB) is needed to start using the app.

Can I use the app offline?
Yes — once a month's audio has been downloaded, it plays without an internet connection.
