What's Inside

Built for the Way You Actually Live

Every detail is designed to get out of the way and let Spurgeon speak.

🎵

High-Quality Narration

All 730 devotions are narrated in a clear, natural voice chosen for comprehension and pacing — making Spurgeon's 19th-century language easier to absorb.

🔒

Background Audio

Audio continues playing after you lock your screen or switch apps. Listen during commutes, walks, or morning routines without interruption.

📅

Always the Right Devotion

The app automatically opens to today's Morning or Evening reading based on the time of day. No navigation needed — just open and read.

🔔

Daily Reminders

Set independent morning and evening notifications at times that fit your schedule. Tapping the alert opens the correct devotion instantly.

🖼️

Home Screen Widget

Small and medium widgets display today's scripture and devotion title at a glance — automatically refreshing each morning and evening.

✈️

Fully Offline

Devotion text is bundled in the app. Audio is cached after first play. Once loaded, everything works with no Wi-Fi or cellular signal required.

🌙

Light & Dark Mode

The app follows your system theme automatically — no setting to change. Clean, readable typography in both light and dark environments.

↗️

Native Sharing

Share any devotion with a tap. The share sheet sends a beautifully formatted message with the scripture, body text, and Spurgeon's attribution.

Start Your Day With Purpose

Free on iOS and Android. No ads, no subscriptions, no account required.
