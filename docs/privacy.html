Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026  ·  Heisforyou

Overview

Morning and Evening does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app functions entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. No account, login, or registration is required to use the app.

Audio Files

Audio narrations are downloaded from a content delivery network (CDN) and cached locally on your device. No identifying information is sent during this process beyond a standard HTTPS request.

Local Storage

The app stores your current reading position and notification preferences locally on your device using standard iOS and Android storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device. No notification data is transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

This app is rated Everyone and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
